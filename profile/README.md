## Appsalon

Appsalon is a Norwegian Shopify and Shopify Plus partner. 

Appsalon offers a range of services to help e-commerce businesses succeed, including custom web development, UX and design strategy, migration and integration of other platforms, including business planning and consulting. 

Drawing upon our expertise within the Shopify ecosystem, Appsalon can help companies both launch and expand their online stores for the future.
